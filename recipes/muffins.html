Easy Chocolate Muffins

Recipe by bakerbettie.com

Description

This is an easy moist chocolate muffin recipe that is filled with chocolate chips. The process of making chocolate muffins is incredibly simple and these muffins come together in less than 30 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 180 grams all-purpose flour
  • 50 grams cocoa powder
  • 130 grams granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 235 ml milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 120 ml vegetable oil
  • 170 grams chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200Â°C
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl, whisk together
  3. Mix the wet ingredients in a separate bowl
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients and stir until just combined - around 10 stirs to complete. Fold in the chocolate chips at this point
  5. Divide the batter between the muffin cups filling them almost all the way up
  6. Bake for about 15 minutes
