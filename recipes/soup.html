Easy Broccoli soup

Recipe by bbcgoodfood.com

Description

Make a comforting bowl of this simple broccoli soup with a handful of ingredients and serve with a side of melty, golden brown cheese toastie bites

Ingredients

  • 1 large potato, diced
  • 1.2l chicken or vegetable stock
  • 400 grams broccoli, chopped

Steps

  1. Chop your potatoes and broccoli
  2. Put the potato and stock in a saucepan and bring to boil, turn down to simmer for 10 minutes until the potato is tender
  3. Add the broccoli and some seasoning, cook for another 4-5 minutes
  4. Blend the soup until smooth
